Menu

Something short and leading about the collection below—its contents, the creator, etc. Make it short and sweet, but not too short so folks don’t simply skip over it entirely.

Brownies

Brownies are a rich, chocolatey dessert bar baked in a square or rectangular pan. They can be fudgy and dense or cake-like and light, depending on the recipe.

Gyoza

Gyoza are Japanese dumplings filled with meat and vegetables, wrapped in thin dough. They can be steamed, pan-fried, or boiled, and are often served with dipping sauce.

Lasagna

Lasagna is an Italian dish made with layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, meat sauce, cheese, and sometimes vegetables, baked in the oven.

Mie Aceh

Mie Aceh is a spicy noodle dish from Aceh, Indonesia. It's known for its rich, savory broth, often flavored with shrimp paste, and a mix of noodles, seafood, and meat.

Ramen

Ramen is a Japanese noodle soup dish featuring wheat noodles, a savory broth, and often topped with various ingredients like sliced pork, seaweed, and green onions.

Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a long, thin pasta typically served with a sauce, such as marinara or meat sauce.
